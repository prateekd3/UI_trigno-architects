About Trigno Architects

Architecture || Interior || Planning

"We shape our homes, and then, our homes shape us" – Winston Churchill
Trigno Architects have been best architect and designer for several years in Raipur, Chhattisgarh. We offer services of Architectural Design, Interior Design, Building Plan (Naksha paas), Turnkey Project Construction Work, Structure Drawing, 3D Elevation View, 3D Interior View, Walkthrough, Vastu Counsultency and Corporation Drawing.
We primarily concerned with the functional arrangement of internal spaces to create effective use and the aesthetic arrangement of the external elements of the building which houses the internal functions, relation to the site and surrounding buildings.
We propose a conceptual picture of a building, which, after long and detailed discussions with a client base, is created to best respond to the project objectives and to create a project budget. And together we have been able to produce magnificent architectural splendors with international standards in Raipur and also other cities of Chhattisgarh.

Mission

Our Mission is to be recognised in the marketplace as a quality driven design services business and focused on servicing our Client Relationships with both creative design and professional project management expertise for projects in both Architecture and Interior.

Vision

Our vision is to be a reputable, desirable premier luxurious architectural and interior designer of the global market place. We will continue to leverage on our many years of professional experience to consistently deliver exclusive design services that bring a complete satisfaction to our clients. We wishes to change the way people see homes, forever.

Quality & Policy

We continually improve and invest in our project quality, working methods, management systems and in our professional staff within our specialist disciplines through creativity and innovation in order to ensure our continual development as a quality driven design services business to our Clients.
